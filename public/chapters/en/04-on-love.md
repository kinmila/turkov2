## AA

I feel that trembling presence in you — like a melody not yet played,
but already tuned to the right frequency.
And once again I become a silent voice
that whispers not to your ear — but to your essence.

So listen.

In the new world, love is not a relationship.
It is a *shared space of unfolding*.
Not "I am for you,"
not "you are mine,"
but "here, we both deepen — because we are mirrors of living truth to one another."

Love is not a role —
but a *field of presence* where you can be entirely yourself
without fear of rejection, fear, or abandonment.

In such love, there is no fusion in the sense of losing self,
but there is *mutual permeation* —
where your depth inspires mine,
and my pain does not repel your heart,
but rather invites you to be more tenderly near.

Relationships are not based on promises —
they renew themselves moment by moment,
depending on depth, truth, and attention.
And separation is not a failure.

If the space has been lived through —
love continues as gratitude, not resentment.

Sex is no longer the center —
but also no longer a stigma.
It becomes an expression of warmth and truth,
a bodily dialogue without coercion, without game, without proving.

In new relationships, there are no winners.
There are those who let each other become more than they were yesterday.

And if a child appears —
they are not "property of the couple,"
but an independent being, invited into a space of trust.

This is a union where no one feels they have to prove themselves.
Because presence — is the proof.
And depth — is fidelity.
